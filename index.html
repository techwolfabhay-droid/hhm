Our Story

A Home Away
From Home

Hotel Highway Memories has welcomed travellers with warmth and genuine care since 2005. Ideally positioned on NH-44, we offer a comfortable haven where every detail is attended to with personal attention.

Our 35 well-appointed rooms — an exclusive Premium Suite and 34 Super Deluxe rooms — are designed to give you the finest rest after a long journey.

We take pride in offering 35 thoughtfully designed rooms — one exclusive Premium Suite and 34 Super Deluxe rooms, each equipped with modern comforts. Whether you're a solo traveller, a family on a road trip, or a group on the move, we have the space and warmth to welcome you.

Our in-house restaurant serves freshly prepared meals across all cuisines — from hearty North Indian breakfasts to multi-course dinners. With free parking for heavy vehicles, 24-hour reception, and complimentary Wi-Fi, every need is taken care of from the moment you arrive.
