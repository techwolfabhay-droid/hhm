About us

Elegance and comfort
on the highway route

Since 2021, Hotel Highway Memories has been a cherished haven for travellers on NH‑44. We blend the warmth of Indian hospitality with thoughtfully appointed comfort — a true home away from home.

"

Our 35 well-appointed rooms are designed to give you the finest rest after a long journey — a sanctuary of comfort on the highway.

Whether you're a solo traveller, a family on a road trip, or a group on the move, we have the space, warmth, and cuisine to welcome you. Free parking, 24‑hour reception, high‑speed Wi‑Fi, and a full multi‑cuisine restaurant await.
