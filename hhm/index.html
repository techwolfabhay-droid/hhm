Our Story

A Home Away From Home

Hotel Highway Memory has welcomed travellers with warmth and genuine care since 2005. Ideally positioned on NH-44 for highway travellers and local guests alike, we offer a comfortable haven where every detail is attended to with personal attention.

Our 35 well-appointed rooms — one exclusive Premium Suite and 34 Super Deluxe rooms — are designed to give you the best rest after a long journey.
